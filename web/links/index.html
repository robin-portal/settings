Plugin Documentation 📜

Generation

World Control

/run [seed]

Generates a new seed (Overworld, Nether, End) in ~ 2 seconds. Leave empty for random or specify a seed.

Lobby

Waiting Room

/lobby

Teleports players to a 10x10 void room with Minecraft borders and a sign to join the active run.

Social

Health Share

/share

Toggle health and food sharing between all players. Status (ON/OFF) is displayed in chat.

Stats

Scoreboard & Logs

Auto-reset timer on start. Chat notifications for Nether/End entry times. Real-time damage and food logs with player names.

🔊 XP sounds triggered for every plugin interaction.
